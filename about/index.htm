A LITTLE ABOUT ME.


The Basics.

Hello, so nice of you to stop by. As you may have guessed by now, my name is Steven Gaffin and I love designing for web and print. I graduated from Chattanooga State Community College in May of 2011 with an Associates Degree in Graphic Design. My educational experience along with my eager love for all things design plays a major role in my work. I see one of my major strengths being my ability to jump easily from being a designer, to a coder, and to a creative thinker. This essential skill makes life easier as a designer and developer.

A Brief History.

My interest in graphic design arose when I was a sophomore in high school. Two friends of mine took me under their wing and taught me the basics about graphic design, web development, and technology. Since then, I have been striving to learn as much as I can about design and web development.
