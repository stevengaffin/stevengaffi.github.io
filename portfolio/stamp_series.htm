Project Information

The Civil Servant Stamp Series is an appreciation of modern day Civil Servants. The goal is to accurately portray the professionalism and bravery of the First Responders who serve in communities across the nation.


A police officer is illustrated in the First Class Stamp. A fire fighter is illustrated in the Additional Postage Stamp. A paramedic is illustrated in the First Class Forever Stamp. Only the Additional Postage Stamp is an engraved stamp.
