Project Information

The Amazing Miles Magic Show is sponsored by Big Brother Big Sister, a non-profit organization. Printed promotional pieces for this project include: a poster, a ticket, an invitation, an envelope, and a premium.


The premium piece is handkerchief which is given out to every person who comes to enjoy the show. Handing out a handkerchief as a gift is sure to spark the imagination of each individual who comes in support the non-profit. A historic theme, the early 20th century, was set for the event and each promotional piece designed.
